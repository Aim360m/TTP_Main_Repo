Michelow Shnitzel receipt

Prep:

Chicken:

Cut chicken cutlets to thin them rather than make strips

Repeat until sufficeint amount

Batter:

1. Whisk 2 eggs in a bowl(varries on how much is being made)

2. On 2 plates, spread panko crumbs on one(optional, but helps with crispiness of outcome), and on the other will be our flour and spice mix.

3. The Mix: In a seperate bowl mix:

flour
paprika
salt

Oil:

Take the oil of your choice and pour into a pot before placing pot on stove

Allow oil to reach a temp

Frying:

Take chicken cutlet and dunk in egg

Pressing into flour, then flip and press other side into flour

battered cutlet into oil

After fry forms remove

Re-egg fryed cutlet and press into panko this time if you chose to use, if not use flour agian

gently lower re-battered fry into oil

Remove once golden
